Peanut Butter Delight

Description:

The Peanut Butter Delight desert was created by my Grandmother. I have been eating this desert ever since I can remember. The Peanut Butter Delight is a luxury of my family, and is only made for my family and some special friends. I have never met anyone who has heard of, or had a Peanut Butter Delight. It is one of a kind.

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Rice Krispies
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Ice Cream

Steps:

  1. Put Peanut Butter and Honey Into a Large Bowl
  2. Microwave the Bowl for 45 Seconds
  3. Mix the Contents of the Bowl Well
  4. Add Rice Krispies and Mix Well
  5. Flatten Mixture, Then Add Chocolate Syrup On Top
  6. Add a Small Amount of Milk
  7. Layer Vanilla Ice Cream On Top
