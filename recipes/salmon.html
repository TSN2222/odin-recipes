Oven Baked Salmon

This is the perfect dish to make for a date. Fresh salmon baked in the oven, topped with tasty seasoning and lemon. It also includes the optional side of asparagus

Ingredients

  • Salmon Filet
  • Lemon
  • Asparagus

Steps

  1. Bake Salmon Filet In Oven
  2. Bake Asparagus In Oven
  3. Put Sliced Lemon On Salmon
  4. Season Salmon
