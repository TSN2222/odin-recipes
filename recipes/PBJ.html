Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich

Description:

The Peanut Butter Jelly Sandwich is a classic and needs no introduction.

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • Bread

Steps:

  1. Grab Two Pieces of Bread
  2. Spread Peanut Butter on One Piece
  3. Spread Jelly on The Other Piece
  4. Press the Two Pieces of Bread Together to Form a Sandwich
