Tuna Melt

Description:

Tuna Melt is a wonderful sandwich that makes for a good lunch.

Ingredients:

  • Bread
  • Tuna
  • Cheese
  • Mayonnaise

Steps:

  1. Grab Two Pieces of Bread
  2. Toast The Bread, and Set Aside
  3. Put a Can of Tuna and Some Mayonnaise Into a Bowl
  4. Mix the Tuna and Mayonnaise Well
  5. Put Cheese on the Toasted Bread
  6. Finally, Put the Tuna and Mayonnaise Mixture on the Bread
  7. Enjoy With a Glass of Milk
