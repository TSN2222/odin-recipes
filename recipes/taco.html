Hardshell Beef Tacos

This taco recipe is a delicious option for those who like to keep things simple. A hardshell filled with ground beef, cheese, and sour cream.

Ingredients

  • Hardshell
  • Ground Beef
  • Taco Blend Cheese
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Cook Ground Beef
  2. Fill Hardshell With All Ingredients
