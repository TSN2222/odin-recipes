Peanut Butter Delight

Home

Description:

A delicious homemade dessert created by my grandma. This recipe has been in our family for many years and is loved by almost everyone who's had the pleasure of trying it. I hope you enjoy it as much as we do!

Ingredients:

  • Peanut Butter
  • Honey
  • Rice Krispies
  • Milk
  • Chocolate Syrup
  • Vanilla Ice Cream

Steps:

  1. Put peanut butter and honey in a bowl
  2. Microwave the bowl for 50 seconds
  3. Mix well once melted
  4. Add Rice Krispies, mix well, then flatten
  5. Add a slash of milk
  6. Drizzle on chocolate syrup
  7. Top it off with a layer of vanilla ice cream
