Pizza Chicken Sandwich

My personal favorite sandwich variation is the Pizza Chicken Sandwich. This sandwich consists of pizza dough for it bread, chicken as it's filling, and a mayonnaise/garlic paste for the spread

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Chicken Breast
  • Mayonnaise
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Bake Pizza Dough In Oven
  2. Cook Chicken Breast On Stove
  3. Mix Mayonnaise And Garlic
  4. Combine All Into Sandwich
